## Christmas Shopping Tips

### Start Early
- Avoid stress and shipping delays
- Better selection
- Time for personalized items
- Spread out the expense

### Make a List
- Everyone you need to buy for
- Budget per person
- Gift ideas as you think of them
- Track what you've purchased

### Set a Budget
- Total holiday spending limit
- Per-person amounts
- Stick to it
- Quality over quantity

### Think Experiences
- Less clutter
- More memories
- Often more meaningful
- Can be any budget
