## 21st Birthday Traditions to Consider

### The Birthday Key

In some cultures, 21 marks receiving the "key to the door"—symbolizing adulthood and independence. A decorative key or key-shaped jewelry honors this tradition.

### 21 Gifts

Give 21 small gifts, one for each year of their life. Can be silly, sentimental, or a mix.

### 21 Reasons

Write out 21 reasons you love them, 21 wishes for their future, or 21 memories you share.

### Birthday Interview

Record a video interview asking about their life, goals, fears, and dreams at 21. They'll treasure watching it years later.

## What to Avoid

**Don't assume everyone wants alcohol-related gifts.** Not everyone drinks, and even those who do might prefer something else.

**Skip generic "21" merchandise.** A shot glass that says "Finally 21!" is forgettable.

**Avoid gifts that feel like obligations.** Gym memberships, self-help books, or anything that implies they need to improve.

**Don't give cash without thought.** If giving money, present it meaningfully—toward a specific goal or experience.
